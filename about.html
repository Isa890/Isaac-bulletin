ABOUT

We are a community of people that wish to ensure the spiritual, social, and health growth of the growing believer in Christ. We are not sentimental about your growth but intentional for you to come into the fullness of Christ. We are here to build your faith by hearing, reading and meditating on the word of God. We are a community of growing christians helping one another in faith towards God's image. We behold him (Christ) until we become just like him.
